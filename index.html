About Us

Alpha Carbons was founded in 2023, during the burgeoning period of the aftermarket industry. Since then, we have risen to become one of the foremost manufacturers and distributors of premium carbon fiber parts and accessories for continental cars. Some of the services we provided but not limited to:

  • Premium Carbon Fiber Parts
  • Headlamp and Tail Lights Conversion
  • Body Kit Upgrades

At Alpha Carbons, we are renowned for our unwavering commitment to excellence, producing products that not only meet but exceed the high expectations of our discerning customers. Our reputation as a trusted brand is a testament to our dedication to offering optimal performance and quality parts for both aesthetic and functional upgrades.
